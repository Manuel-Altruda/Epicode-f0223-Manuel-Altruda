Focaccia with Caramelized Onions
Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweer Caramelized onions, tart
cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

    for the focaccia
  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yest
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukeworm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plud more for the pan
    for the topping
  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes(scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cup fresh or frozen cranberries
Method
  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment
mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough,
though i sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
